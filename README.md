# Marble EU Crypto AML Rule Pack

EU AML rule pack for the Marble open-source compliance engine, comprising seven scenarios aligned to FATF Recommendations 10, 12, 15, 16, and 19, EU MiCA Articles 67 and 68, EU Transfer of Funds Regulation 2023/1113 Article 14, AMLD6 Articles 13 and 23, and Turkish MASAK Law no. 5549.

## Status

Work in progress. Estimated completion: June 2026.

## Scenarios

1. High-Velocity Structuring Detection
2. Mixer and Tumbler Exposure Screening
3. Travel Rule Pre-Block (EU TFR)
4. PEP Onboarding Risk Escalation
5. Sanctioned Counterparty Screening
6. Smurfing Network Detection
7. MASAK 5549 Cross-Border Suspicious Activity

## Data sources

- Elliptic Bitcoin Dataset (Weber et al., 2019) for illicit transaction patterns
- OpenSanctions consolidated dataset for sanctions and PEP screening
- Synthetic data generated for KYC, KYB, and behavioral patterns
